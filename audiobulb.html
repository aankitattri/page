Discovery

No app.
No trust.
No scale.

Audiobulb is a music publishing company that helps musicians protect and collect royalties from across the globe. But their entire operation ran on Google Forms, WhatsApp, and email — with zero visibility for artists into their own earnings.

Before any design work could begin, the existing system needed to be mapped and its failure points understood.

Status Before Revamping
  • Submissions accepted through Google Forms — no app or website
  • Artists emailed every time they needed a status update
  • Email replies sometimes took more than 24 hours
  • Earnings shared via spreadsheets over WhatsApp — a multi-step frustrating process
Pain Points & Results
01
Trust
No website or mobile app
No digital presence gave the impression of an unestablished or unreliable company to new and existing artists.
Result → Trust issues with the company
02
Visibility
No real-time streams or earnings
Artists had to reload a WhatsApp spreadsheet to see their streaming data — too many steps, too much friction, too much waiting.
Result → Frustration and disengagement
03
Support
No convenient customer support
Every query required an email. Response times were long, and artists sometimes felt their issues were being ignored.
Result → Artists felt defrauded
